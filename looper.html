Features

Why Looper

Designed for the solo performer who needs a second pair of hands.

🦶

Stomp-to-Loop

Start recording, overdub, and playback with simple foot taps. No bending down, no hand controls. Pure hands-free workflow.

🎸

Built for Guitar

Designed specifically for guitarists who need to build layers live. Record a chord progression, then solo over it.

🎤

Vocal Looping

Beatboxers and vocalists can build full arrangements one layer at a time. Create harmonies, rhythms, and melodies live.

🖥️

Simple UI

One big button. No menus to navigate during a performance. The interface is designed to be operated by your foot at a glance.

🔊

Unlimited Layers

Stack as many layers as your creativity demands. Build dense, complex arrangements from a single instrument.

⚡

Zero Latency

Your loops sync perfectly. No drift, no delay between layers. What you hear is what you played.
