Studio
Drum. Loop. FX. Record.

Studio expands the Stompai workflow into a single performance screen with song banks, heel and toe kick assignments, live FX, audio input routing, metronome control, and onboard recording.

Release Day: June 1

Built for Performance

Everything on One Stage-Ready Surface

The new layout keeps sound selection, playback control, live processing, and master settings visible at the same time.

Song banks

Load and switch between songs from a dedicated bank column without breaking your setup flow.

Heel + toe zones

Assign distinct sounds to heel kick and toe kick lanes for more expressive foot performance.

FX and input

Route external audio into the FX channel and shape the sound from inside the same performance view.

Master control

Access metronome, recording, volume, and transport controls without leaving the main screen.

Why Studio

A Bigger Live Workflow

The screenshots point to a more complete rig for performers who need percussion, processing, and capture in one place.

01

Multi-column performance view

Bank, kick lanes, FX, and master controls stay visible together so you can make decisions fast while playing.

02

Assignable foot sounds

Build song-specific kits by selecting different hits and assigning them to each active zone.

03

Live FX processing

Drive, dry/wet mix, feedback, and timing controls make the FX section useful as part of the performance, not an afterthought.

04

Audio device selection

Choose built-in or USB audio sources directly in the app when routing guitar or other external signals.

05

Song recording

Open recording controls from the master section and manage saved songs without leaving the main interface.

06

Integrated tempo control

Metronome and tap controls sit next to the master lane so timing stays central to the whole setup.

Inside Studio

Main Performance Screen

FX Settings

Audio Input Routing

Recording and Song Management
