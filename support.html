DoubleKick Setup

Get the DoubleKick app configured and playing in minutes.

  • Download DoubleKick from the App Store
  • Attach your phone to the Barker Edition Mount or Pro Pedal
  • Open the app and select your drum kit
  • Adjust sensitivity to your playing style
  • Stomp to play — heel kick, toe kick, or both
Download DoubleKick

Looper Setup

Start hands-free looping with your guitar or vocals.

  • Download Looper from the App Store
  • Mount your phone in the Guitar Looper Mount at your feet
  • Connect your instrument or use the built-in mic
  • Stomp once to start recording your first loop
  • Stomp again to overdub — stack unlimited layers

🔧 Hardware Setup

Install and maintain your Stompai mounts and pedals.

  • MagSafe Mount: Align your iPhone with the mount — magnets snap into place automatically
  • Pro Drum Pedal: Place on a flat surface, mount phone in the holder, adjust angle
  • Looper Mount: Position near your pedalboard, secure phone, angle for visibility
  • Rubber Bands: Stretch over the mount arms to add extra security for vigorous playing
